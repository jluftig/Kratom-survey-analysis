Clinical Takeaways

1
Screen aggressively — kratom/7-OH not on standard UDS. Ask everyone.
2
Differentiate by product — leaf = treat like mild-moderate OUD; 7-OH = treat like fentanyl OUD.
3
Buprenorphine is first-line, highly effective, and overwhelmingly preferred — methadone rarely used.
