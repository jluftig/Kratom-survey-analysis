Bup Starts & Adjuncts

  • ✔
    COWS ≥8 most common threshold (many use clinical judgment)
  • ✔
    Flexible bup starts: micro-dosing or high-dose (16–32 mg Day 1) both used
  • ⚠
    Precipitated withdrawal reported by 22% (10/45) of clinicians — predominantly among higher-volume clinicians performing more buprenorphine starts
  • ✔
    Adjuncts nearly universal: clonidine, ondansetron, hydroxyzine, gabapentin, methocarbamol

Clinical Takeaways

1
Screen aggressively — kratom/7-OH not on standard UDS. Ask everyone.
2
Differentiate by product — leaf products = moderate OUD; 7-OH = high-potency, treat like fentanyl OUD.
3
Buprenorphine is first-line and highly effective.
4
Buprenorphine overwhelmingly preferred — Methadone rarely used.
